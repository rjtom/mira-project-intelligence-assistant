# ForgeNova Software-Defined Vehicle (SDV) Platform 2027

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q1 2025 – Q4 2027 (36 months)  
**Status:** Planning Phase

## Overview
Development of a next-generation Software-Defined Vehicle platform that enables continuous feature updates and personalized user experiences.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Create a flexible and updatable vehicle architecture
- Deliver meaningful value to drivers over the vehicle lifecycle
- Maintain the highest standards of safety and security

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2025: Architecture design and foundational development
- 2026: Integration, testing, and pilot programs
- 2027: Production launch and initial deployments

## Resource Requirements
- Large cross-functional team of software, hardware, and safety engineers

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Regular safety and ethics review boards
- Human judgment gates for major architectural decisions

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That continuous updates would always improve the customer experience
- Perception & Reality Check: How will constant connectivity and updates affect owners’ sense of ownership?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this platform truly enhance human mobility and freedom?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Technology should serve human beings over time, not make them dependent on constant digital intervention.
