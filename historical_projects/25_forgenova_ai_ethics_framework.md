# ForgeNova AI Ethics and Governance Framework

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q4 2025 – Q4 2026 (24 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 1)

## Overview
Development of a comprehensive AI ethics framework to guide responsible development and deployment.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Embed ethical considerations into all AI projects
- Build internal capability in responsible AI
- Earn and maintain public trust

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2026: Framework development and pilot application

## Resource Requirements
- Ethics, legal, technical, and business stakeholders

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Independent ethics review board

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That technical excellence alone was sufficient
- Perception & Reality Check: How will these decisions affect society?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this framework genuinely prioritize human well-being?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Technology without ethics eventually harms the very people it claims to serve.
