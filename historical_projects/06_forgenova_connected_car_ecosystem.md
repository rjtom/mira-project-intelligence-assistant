# ForgeNova Connected Car Ecosystem Platform

**Project Type:** Automotive OEM  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q2 2024 – Q1 2027 (32 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 1)

## Overview
Creation of an integrated connected vehicle ecosystem focusing on safety, convenience, and seamless user experience.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Deliver meaningful value to drivers and passengers
- Maintain strong privacy and security standards
- Build lasting customer relationships

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024: Platform architecture and core features
- 2025: Integration and user testing
- 2026–2027: Full rollout and continuous improvement

## Resource Requirements
- Software, UX, and data science teams
- Strong privacy and security governance

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Privacy and ethics review committees
- Regular user feedback integration

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That more connectivity always equals better experience
- Perception & Reality Check: How does constant connectivity affect human attention and well-being?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this technology enhance or diminish human connection?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Technology should serve human connection and well-being, not distract from it.
