# ForgeNova Autonomous Driving Platform Development

**Project Type:** Automotive OEM  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q2 2023 – Q1 2025 (21 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 3)

## Overview
Development of a Level 4 autonomous driving system emphasizing safety, sensor fusion, and real-world reliability.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Achieve high safety performance in complex environments
- Build trust through transparent development
- Balance innovation with responsible deployment

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2023: Architecture and requirements definition
- 2024: Core development and extensive simulation testing
- Q1 2025: Real-world validation and regulatory preparation

## Resource Requirements
- 380+ AI/ML engineers and safety specialists
- Strong collaboration with regulatory bodies

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Regular safety and ethics review boards
- Human oversight at critical validation stages

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That simulation results would fully match real human experiences
- Perception & Reality Check: How might this technology affect human drivers and passengers?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this work prioritize human safety and dignity above speed to market?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: In complex systems involving human lives, humility and rigorous validation are more valuable than rapid deployment.
