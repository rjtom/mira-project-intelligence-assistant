# ForgeNova Enterprise Sustainability Dashboard

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q2 2025 – Q1 2027 (21 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 1)

## Overview
Unified dashboard providing real-time visibility into environmental and social impact metrics.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Enable better informed sustainability decisions
- Foster transparency with stakeholders
- Drive meaningful improvements over time

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2025: Data integration and baseline development
- 2026: Advanced analytics and reporting

## Resource Requirements
- Sustainability, data, and business intelligence teams

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Regular ethics and impact review sessions

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That measuring more metrics would automatically lead to better outcomes
- Perception & Reality Check: How do these metrics affect real communities and ecosystems?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this dashboard encourage genuine care for the planet and people?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Measurement is only valuable when paired with wisdom and the will to act.
