# ForgeNova Urban Air Mobility Initiative

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q3 2025 – Q2 2028 (34 months)  
**Status:** Concept Phase

## Overview
Exploration of electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) solutions for urban air mobility.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Develop safe and accessible air mobility options
- Integrate responsibly with existing transportation systems
- Prioritize community acceptance and safety

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2025–2026: Concept design and regulatory engagement
- 2027: Prototype development and testing
- 2028: Pilot program preparation

## Resource Requirements
- Aerospace, regulatory, and urban planning specialists

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Community impact and safety oversight boards

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That air mobility would be universally beneficial
- Perception & Reality Check: How will this affect people living near flight paths?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this expand human freedom without creating new burdens?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Mobility solutions must respect both those who benefit and those who are impacted.
