# ForgeNova EV Battery Gigafactory Expansion - Phase 2

**Project Type:** Automotive OEM  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q1 2024 – Q4 2024 (12 months)  
**Status:** Completed Successfully

## Overview
This project focused on doubling the production capacity of ForgeNova’s flagship EV battery gigafactory while integrating next-generation automation and sustainable manufacturing processes. It was a significant step in supporting the transition to electric mobility.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Double production capacity while maintaining high quality standards
- Achieve strong safety and environmental performance
- Deliver meaningful contribution to sustainable transportation
- Balance speed of execution with care for team well-being

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- **Q1 2024**: Infrastructure upgrades and equipment installation
- **Q2 2024**: Technology integration and initial pilot testing
- **Q3 2024**: Workforce training and production ramp-up
- **Q4 2024**: Full-scale production and final certification

## Resource Requirements
- Approximately 450 team members across engineering, manufacturing, and quality
- Significant capital investment
- Collaboration with multiple technology partners

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Monthly leadership reviews with cross-functional input
- Key decision points requiring human judgment on safety and quality
- Regular reflection sessions on team impact and workload

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That new technology would integrate smoothly without affecting people
- Perception & Reality Check: How do these changes affect the daily lives of workers and their families?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this project honor human dignity and long-term societal benefit?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Projects succeed not just through technology, but through careful attention to human realities, transparent decision-making, and maintaining space for human judgment throughout the journey.
