# ForgeNova Enterprise Cybersecurity Enhancement Program

**Project Type:** Internal IT  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q3 2024 – Q2 2026 (22 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 1)

## Overview
Strengthening enterprise-wide cybersecurity capabilities to protect intellectual property and operational integrity.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Build robust defense while maintaining usability
- Foster a culture of security awareness
- Protect both company assets and customer trust

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024: Assessment and foundational improvements
- 2025: Advanced tooling and training
- 2026: Continuous monitoring and maturity

## Resource Requirements
- Cybersecurity specialists and cross-functional security champions

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Regular ethics and privacy reviews
- Human-led incident response simulations

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That technology alone could solve security challenges
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this program affect employee experience and trust?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this protection come with respect for privacy and human dignity?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Security is most effective when it works with people, not against them.
