# ForgeNova AI-Powered Quality Control System

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q4 2025 – Q3 2027 (33 months)  
**Status:** Development Phase

## Overview
Advanced AI system for real-time quality detection and process optimization in manufacturing.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Improve quality while reducing waste
- Support workers rather than replace them
- Maintain human oversight in critical decisions

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2026: System development and pilot lines
- 2027: Full integration and refinement

## Resource Requirements
- AI vision specialists and manufacturing experts

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Human validation loops for AI decisions

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That AI would always outperform human inspectors
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this system affect the dignity and role of quality workers?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this technology support or diminish human craftsmanship?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: The best AI augments human capability and respect.
