# ForgeNova AI Driver Assistance System

**Project Type:** Automotive OEM  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q3 2023 – Q4 2025 (28 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 2)

## Overview
Development of advanced AI-powered driver assistance features focused on safety and human augmentation.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Enhance driver safety without replacing human judgment
- Create intuitive and trustworthy systems
- Maintain driver agency and situational awareness

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2023–2024: Core AI development and testing
- 2025: Integration, validation, and deployment

## Resource Requirements
- AI safety specialists and human factors experts

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Human factors and safety review boards
- Continuous monitoring of real-world performance

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That AI would always make better decisions than humans
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this system affect driver confidence and attention?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this technology truly support and protect human drivers?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: The best AI assistance augments human capability while preserving human dignity and responsibility.
