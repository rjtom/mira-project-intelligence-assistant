# ForgeNova Global ERP System Migration

**Project Type:** Internal IT  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q2 2024 – Q1 2026 (21 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 2)

## Overview
Comprehensive migration of global operations to a modern ERP platform to improve visibility, efficiency, and decision-making.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Achieve unified data visibility across regions
- Improve operational efficiency while minimizing disruption
- Support better decision-making for people across the organization

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- Q2-Q4 2024: Assessment and planning
- 2025: Phased implementation and data migration
- Q1 2026: Full rollout and optimization

## Resource Requirements
- Cross-functional teams including finance, operations, and IT
- Strong change management support

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Regular business unit impact reviews
- Human oversight during data migration cutovers
- Team feedback sessions on system usability

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That the new system would be immediately embraced
- Perception & Reality Check: How will this change affect daily work and stress levels of employees?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this migration ultimately serve the people who will use it every day?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Technical migrations succeed when equal attention is given to systems and the humans who must adopt them.
