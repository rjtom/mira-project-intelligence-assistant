# ForgeNova Enterprise Data Analytics Platform

**Project Type:** Internal IT  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q4 2023 – Q3 2025 (21 months)  
**Status:** Completed Successfully

## Overview
Development of a unified data platform to enable better insights across the organization.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Deliver actionable insights responsibly
- Maintain strong data governance and privacy
- Empower teams to make better decisions

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024: Platform foundation and key integrations
- 2025: Advanced analytics and adoption

## Resource Requirements
- Data engineers, analysts, and business stakeholders

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Data ethics review committee
- Regular impact assessments on decision-making processes

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That more data always leads to better decisions
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this platform affect human intuition and judgment?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this tool support wise and compassionate decision-making?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Data is most powerful when combined with human wisdom and ethical consideration.
