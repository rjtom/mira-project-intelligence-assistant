# ForgeNova Blockchain-Enabled Supply Chain Transparency

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q1 2026 – Q4 2027 (24 months)  
**Status:** Planning Phase

## Overview
Implementation of blockchain technology to increase traceability and ethical sourcing across the supply chain.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Improve transparency without compromising competitiveness
- Support more responsible sourcing decisions
- Build greater trust with customers and partners

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2026: Platform design and pilot with key suppliers
- 2027: Broader rollout and integration

## Resource Requirements
- Blockchain specialists and supply chain experts

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Ethical sourcing and human rights review committees

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That technology would automatically ensure ethical behavior
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this affect workers and communities in the supply chain?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this promote fairness and dignity for all involved?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Transparency tools are only as good as the human commitment behind them.
