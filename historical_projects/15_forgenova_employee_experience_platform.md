# ForgeNova Employee Experience Platform

**Project Type:** Internal IT  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q3 2024 – Q2 2026 (22 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 1)

## Overview
Development of an integrated platform to improve employee experience, collaboration, and well-being.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Create more seamless and supportive work experiences
- Reduce administrative burden
- Foster better connection across global teams

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024: Needs assessment and design
- 2025: Implementation and adoption
- 2026: Optimization

## Resource Requirements
- HR, IT, and change management teams

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Regular employee feedback loops

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That new tools would automatically improve experience
- Perception & Reality Check: How do these tools affect daily stress and fulfillment?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this platform genuinely support human flourishing at work?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Technology should reduce suffering and enhance human connection in the workplace.
