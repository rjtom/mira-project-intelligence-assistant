# ForgeNova Global Supply Chain Visibility Platform

**Project Type:** Internal IT  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q1 2024 – Q4 2025 (24 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 2)

## Overview
Implementation of end-to-end supply chain visibility platform to improve resilience and decision-making.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Increase transparency across the supply network
- Improve response to disruptions
- Support more ethical and sustainable sourcing

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024: Platform integration and pilot
- 2025: Full rollout and advanced analytics

## Resource Requirements
- Supply chain, IT, and sustainability teams

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Ethical sourcing and risk review meetings

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That visibility alone would solve all supply chain issues
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this affect partners and workers in the supply chain?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this promote fairness and care across the entire ecosystem?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: True resilience comes from seeing the human reality behind every link in the chain.
