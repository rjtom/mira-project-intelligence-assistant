# ForgeNova Autonomous Logistics Network

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q1 2026 – Q4 2028 (36 months)  
**Status:** Concept Phase

## Overview
Development of autonomous systems for internal and supplier logistics operations.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Improve efficiency and safety in logistics
- Reduce physical strain on workers
- Maintain human oversight in complex situations

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2026–2027: Technology development and testing
- 2028: Pilot deployment

## Resource Requirements
- Robotics, logistics, and safety specialists

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Worker impact and safety review boards

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That automation would always improve working conditions
- Perception & Reality Check: How will this change the nature of work for logistics teams?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this reduce suffering while creating meaningful new roles?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Automation should liberate people, not merely displace them.
