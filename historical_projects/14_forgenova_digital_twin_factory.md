# ForgeNova Digital Twin Factory Initiative

**Project Type:** Internal IT  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q2 2024 – Q1 2027 (32 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 1)

## Overview
Implementation of digital twin technology for manufacturing plants to improve efficiency and predictive maintenance.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Reduce downtime and improve quality
- Support data-driven operational decisions
- Enhance safety through better visibility

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024–2025: Pilot implementation
- 2026–2027: Full factory rollout

## Resource Requirements
- IoT, simulation, and operations specialists

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Safety and operational impact reviews

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That digital models would perfectly represent physical reality
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this technology affect workers on the factory floor?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this improve working conditions and human safety?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Digital tools are most valuable when they enhance, rather than replace, human understanding of physical systems.
