# ForgeNova Hydrogen Fuel Cell Vehicle Program

**Project Type:** Automotive OEM  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q4 2023 – Q3 2026 (33 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 1)

## Overview
Development of hydrogen fuel cell technology as a complementary solution for heavy-duty and long-range mobility.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Achieve efficient and clean hydrogen propulsion
- Ensure safety and reliability in real-world conditions
- Contribute to a diverse sustainable mobility ecosystem

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024: Core technology development
- 2025: Prototype testing and refinement
- 2026: Pilot deployment and validation

## Resource Requirements
- Specialized chemical and powertrain engineers
- Infrastructure and safety partners

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Safety and environmental impact reviews
- Cross-functional decision points with human judgment

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That one technology (battery or hydrogen) would solve all challenges
- Perception & Reality Check: How will this technology serve different human needs and contexts?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this expand options for sustainable mobility in a caring way?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: True innovation often lies in embracing multiple paths with open-mindedness and respect for diverse real-world needs.
