# ForgeNova Battery Recycling & Second-Life Program

**Project Type:** Automotive OEM  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q1 2024 – Q4 2026 (36 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 2)

## Overview
Development of a comprehensive battery recycling and second-life energy storage program.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Maximize material recovery and circular economy impact
- Create safe and efficient recycling processes
- Generate value from second-life applications

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024: Process development and pilot facilities
- 2025: Scale-up and optimization
- 2026: Full operations and partnerships

## Resource Requirements
- Chemical engineers, logistics, and sustainability teams

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Environmental and safety oversight
- Community impact assessments

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That recycling would be straightforward at scale
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this program affect workers and local communities?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this reflect responsible stewardship of resources?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Sustainability requires long-term thinking, humility about challenges, and genuine care for environmental and human impact.
