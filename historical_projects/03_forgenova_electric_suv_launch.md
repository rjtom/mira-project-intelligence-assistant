# ForgeNova Electric SUV Launch Program

**Project Type:** Automotive OEM  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q3 2023 – Q4 2025 (27 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 2)

## Overview
Development and market launch of a new flagship electric SUV designed for family use, combining performance, safety, and sustainability.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Deliver an excellent driving and ownership experience
- Meet rigorous safety and quality standards
- Build customer trust through transparent communication
- Balance innovation with practical usability

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- Q3-Q4 2023: Concept design and market research
- 2024: Engineering development and prototype testing
- 2025: Production ramp-up and market launch

## Resource Requirements
- Cross-functional team of designers, engineers, and marketing specialists
- Strong collaboration with supply chain partners

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Regular customer insight reviews
- Safety and quality decision gates with human oversight
- Team well-being check-ins during high-pressure phases

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That customers would prioritize features over reliability
- Perception & Reality Check: How will this vehicle fit into real family lives and daily needs?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this project truly serve people’s desire for safe, sustainable mobility?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Understanding real human needs and maintaining humility throughout development leads to more meaningful products.
