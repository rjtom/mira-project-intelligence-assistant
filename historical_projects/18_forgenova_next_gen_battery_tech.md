# ForgeNova Next-Generation Battery Technology Program

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q2 2025 – Q4 2028 (42 months)  
**Status:** Research Phase

## Overview
Research and development of breakthrough battery technologies beyond current lithium-ion limitations.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Achieve major improvements in energy density and safety
- Develop more sustainable and ethically sourced materials
- Prepare for responsible commercialization

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2025–2026: Fundamental research and lab testing
- 2027: Prototype development and validation
- 2028: Scale-up preparation

## Resource Requirements
- Advanced materials scientists and battery engineers

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Ethics committee for material sourcing and environmental impact

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That higher performance always justifies the trade-offs
- Perception & Reality Check: How will new materials affect mining communities and the environment?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this innovation reflect responsible stewardship?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: True technological progress must consider its full impact on people and the planet.
