# ForgeNova Multi-Cloud Infrastructure Modernization

**Project Type:** Internal IT  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q1 2024 – Q4 2025 (24 months)  
**Status:** In Progress (Phase 2)

## Overview
Modernization of IT infrastructure to a secure and flexible multi-cloud environment.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Improve scalability and resilience
- Maintain strong security and compliance
- Support innovation while ensuring stability

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2024: Strategy, assessment, and pilot migrations
- 2025: Full migration and optimization

## Resource Requirements
- Cloud architects, security specialists, and operations teams

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Security and risk review boards
- Business continuity planning with human judgment

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That moving to cloud would automatically solve all infrastructure issues
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this affect the daily experience of development and operations teams?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this create a more supportive foundation for the people building future products?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: Infrastructure decisions should ultimately serve human creativity and reliability.
