# ForgeNova Circular Economy Platform

**Project Type:** Future Strategic  
**Company:** ForgeNova Automotive  
**Timeline:** Q3 2025 – Q2 2028 (34 months)  
**Status:** Planning Phase

## Overview
Comprehensive platform to support vehicle lifecycle circularity from design to end-of-life.

## Objectives & Success Criteria
- Maximize material recovery and reuse
- Design products with circularity in mind
- Create new value streams from returned materials

## Detailed Timeline & Milestones
- 2026: Platform design and partner ecosystem
- 2027–2028: Implementation and scaling

## Resource Requirements
- Sustainability, design, and supply chain teams

## Governance & HIL Checkpoints
- Long-term impact assessment reviews

## Critical Thinking Analysis
- Assumptions Challenged: That linear models could continue indefinitely
- Perception & Reality Check: How does this affect future generations?
- Love as Derived Work: Does this reflect responsible care for the world we leave behind?

## Lessons Learned
A reflection of genuine insight: True innovation considers the full lifecycle and legacy of what we create.
